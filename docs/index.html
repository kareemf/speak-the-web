Speak the Web

Web articles read aloud.

Features

Safari Share Extension

No copy-paste required. From Safari, just tap Share and select the app.

Natural Voices

Choose from Apple's built-in voices or download high-quality, offline, on-device voice models.

Table of Contents

Navigate articles by section. Tap headings to jump directly to that part of the content.

Start Reading From Anywhere

Select text in Text mode and use "Start Reading From Anywhere" to jump to any point in an article. Especially useful when articles do not have semantic headers for a table of contents.

Recent Articles

Your reading history with progress tracking. Resume where you left off. Text and generated speech are cached for offline listening

Playback Controls

Skip forward/back, adjust speed from 0.5x to 2x, and seek to any part of the article.

Background Playback

Keep listening after you switch apps or lock your phone. Works with Lock Screen controls.

Privacy, Respected

No telemetry, no ads, no tracking. Processing happens on-device

Start Listening Today

Download Speak the Web and turn your reading list into a listening list.
